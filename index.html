AI enables people with disabilities to step into a world where their difficulties are understood and considered. Technology adapts and helps transform the world into an inclusive place with artificial intelligence accessibility.

Our aim to create a smart wheelchair with the help of installing a PI motherboard to the back of the chair with additional features such as a microphone, speaker. [Possible features include a camera for facial imprint and recognize people and objects, as well as motors to control the wheelchair]

About

PROJECT P.A.R.A.S

PROJECT P.A.R.A.S is a smart companion wheelchair for the paralytic and disable people who use wheelchairs just to actually move around. Its is powered by the TARS AI developed in python code, designed by the project's team.
